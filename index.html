About Swami Karunanidhi

Swami Karunanidhi is one of the Panch Ratan of Siddhashram and is the guiding light of Nikhilam Wellness Centre. He possesses a deep understanding of ancient Hindu Scriptures and Vedas.

His areas of knowledge encompass Astrology, Vaastu, Palmistry, Numerology, Ayurveda, and Spiritual Healing. Numerous individuals have found positive changes in their lives through his wise counsel.

Swami Karunanidhi excels in explaining complex concepts simply and offers tailor-made solutions to individual problems.

If you’re seeking to bring clarity and peace into your life, his guidance offers an enriching path.
